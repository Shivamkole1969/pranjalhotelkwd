Our Story

A Legacy of Pure Vegetarian Excellence

Welcome to Hotel Pranjal, Kurduwadi's most beloved pure vegetarian restaurant. Nestled in the heart of Bagal Nagar on Madha Road, we have been serving authentic Indian cuisine with love and passion for over 14 years.

Our commitment to quality, hygiene, and taste has earned us a remarkable 4.7-star rating from over 1500+ happy customers. Every dish we serve is prepared with the finest ingredients, traditional recipes, and modern culinary techniques.
